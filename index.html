    A service that delivers groceries, and need-based supplies to elderly and people with immunodeficiencies in the Davis community.

    How It Works

    How It Works
    1
    Fill out an order form
    Click the button above and fill out the google form with your order, you will get a confirmation message once we receive your order.
    2
    Delivery
    Your items will be collected masks and gloves, then you will receive a picture of your receipt as well as an invoice for the amount that shows up. There is no delivery fee, just pay however much your groceries cost!
    3
    Enjoy!
    Your items will be thoroughly sanitized and then delivered to your house after payment is received.
